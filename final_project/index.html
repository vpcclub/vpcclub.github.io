Mission

The Victoria Police Cricket Club participates in various one day cricket formats and community events throughout the summer months designed to enhance the relationship between Victoria Police and the wider community.

We engage directly with the culturally, linguistically and religiously diverse communities to encourage camaraderie, physical fitness and project a favourable image of Victoria Police.

Additionally we seek to to promote sportsmanship, a sense of fair play and the game of cricket itself.

Members

Membership is available to all sworn and unsworn members of Victoria Police and there is no pre-requisite skill level needed to be considered for team selection.

This year we are particularly excited to launch the first Victoria Police Cricket Club women's team, which creates an opportunity for female staff to also participate in organised matches.
