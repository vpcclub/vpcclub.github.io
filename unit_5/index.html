Our Mission

The Victoria Police Cricket Club participates in community engagement events designed to enhance the relationship between Victoria Police and the wider community.

We engage directly with the culturally, linguistically and religiously diverse communities to encourage camaraderie, physical fitness and project a favourable image of Victoria Police. Additionally we seek to to promote sportsmanship, a sense of fair play and the game of cricket itself.

Membership

The Victoria Police Cricket Club plays various cricket formats throughout the summer months. The club is available to all sworn and unsworn members of Victoria Police and there is no pre-requisite to be considered for team selection.
